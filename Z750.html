A Letter to Z750

2021-06-23

Foreword

Two days ago Z750 publicly announced his plans of leaving PaperMC and the Minecraft development community as a whole. You can read that announcement here: https://zachbr.io/posts/2021-06-22-leaving-papermc-and-minecraft-dev/

This wasn't news to the Core Team, but it’s still a bittersweet day. Z750 co-founded Paper and has spent several years with the Core Team building Paper to what it is today, and we have all formed deep friendships with him.

To Z750,

Thank you for all of your work, time, and your friendship. You have always been a major part of the Paper project and community - and you always will be, even after you’ve left. The impact you’ve had on this project and community is immeasurable. That being said, very few people get to say that something they've built from the ground up grew into something really big, used by thousands of people - and to be able to pass that on to others to continue. That’s a huge accomplishment. We wish you the best of luck in whatever you do after Paper, and know we’ll always be here if you ever want to chat.
– The Core Team

I will miss having you to help defend the name of the taco, and for support of the taco supremacy over burritos. We still need to meet up and have some tacos, but until then, have one for me! You will be missed.
– Aikar

I've had an amazing time working on Paper, and I'm amazed where the project has gone since I joined almost 6 years ago, so thank you for letting me be a part of this. I'm with Aikar though - we definitely need to meet up and get tacos. In the meantime, keep listening to Taylor! Best of luck.
– DenWav

5 years ago, according to NickServ, I for some reason decided to get myself tangled back up in the Minecraft community, and god knows how long later joined Spigots IRC team and getting into the spirit of the community and adopting the staff curse, to one day figuring out that I’d take a peek at whatever the heck was as frowned upon as glancing at a burrito, and ended up finding a little community which helped me to hone on and joined the Paper team what must have been a year later. While somewhat late to the club, the ride was definitely wild, and I was very glad to have spent that time and made friends with somebody as… exquisite, as yourself, as well as the community which once was a small trickle until it all exploded. The journey so far has been a fun, if not interesting one, and it blows you’re not gonna be here as we continue on this quest, but, it’s been great, frendino, and I hope that you do well and continue to drop on by, even if you only manage once in a blue moon
– electroniccat

We have had some fun years working together - back when Paper was just a tiny project used by a few people, to where it is now. I will miss having you around.
– kashike

While we haven't worked directly for as long as some others here, you were one of the first people I ever interacted with in this community, I don't know how many years ago back in irc times. It's really strange to think about you not being as present anymore, but you for sure have left a mark on the community and me personally, especially the love for tacos.
– MiniDigger
